Tianli Liao

I am a lecturer in the College of Information Science and Engineering, Henan University of Technology. I received my Ph.D. degree in Applied Mathematics in 2019 from the Center for Combinatorics, Nankai University, supervised by William Y.C. Chen. My current research interests mainly lie in the fields of deep learning, computer vision, and image processing, especially image/video stitching, image manipulation, and neural rendering.
